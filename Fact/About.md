# Anki-Cards/Fact
A flashcard for a fact.

## Fields

|Name|Represents|
|:--|:--|
|Question|The incomplete fact. Use `<ac-placeholder/>` to put in a placeholder.|
|Fact|The complete fact.|
|Explanation|The explanation. (Optional)|
