# Anki-Cards/Definition
A flashcard for dictionary or terminology definitions.

## Fields

|Name|Represents|
|:--|:--|
|Term|The word or term being defined.|
|Definition|The definition.|
|Reverse|If not empty, will generate a reversed card.|
